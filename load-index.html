Tire Load Index and Speed Rating Explained

The load index is a number on the tire sidewall that corresponds to a maximum weight capacity per tire. It is NOT the weight itself — you must look it up in a table.

The speed rating is a letter that indicates the maximum sustained speed the tire is designed for. Always match or exceed your vehicle manufacturer's recommended load index and speed rating.
