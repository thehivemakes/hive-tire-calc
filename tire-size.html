How to Compare Tire Sizes

Tire size is expressed as width/aspect ratio R rim diameter (e.g., 225/45R17). The width is in millimeters, the aspect ratio is the sidewall height as a percentage of width, and the rim diameter is in inches.

When changing tire sizes, keep the overall diameter within 3% of original to avoid speedometer errors, ABS issues, and potential rubbing. This calculator shows exact differences.
