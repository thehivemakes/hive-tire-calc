Common vehicles —
PCD (Pitch Circle Diameter) is the diameter of the circle formed by the bolt holes. Common patterns: 5x114.3 (most Japanese/Korean), 5x112 (most German), 5x120 (BMW), 6x139.7 (trucks).

Understanding Wheel Bolt Patterns

A bolt pattern is described as number of bolts × PCD in millimeters (e.g., 5x114.3). Both must match for a wheel to fit your vehicle.

Never force a wheel with the wrong bolt pattern. Even a 1mm difference in PCD means the wheel is not properly centered and can vibrate or loosen.
