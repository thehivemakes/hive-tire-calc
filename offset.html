Understanding Wheel Offset

Wheel offset (ET) determines how far in or out the wheel sits relative to the hub. Positive offset tucks the wheel inward; negative offset pushes it outward.

When changing wheels, compare the new offset to stock. A difference of more than 10-15mm can affect handling, cause rubbing, or put stress on wheel bearings.
