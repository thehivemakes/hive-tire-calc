How Tire Size Affects Your Speedometer

Your speedometer is calibrated for your factory tire size. When you change tire sizes, the speedometer becomes inaccurate because the number of wheel revolutions per mile changes.

Larger tires travel farther per revolution, so the speedometer reads lower than actual speed. This calculator tells you your true speed at any indicated reading.
